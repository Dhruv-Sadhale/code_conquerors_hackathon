Finding the Right Fit

The various clubs of CoEP provide the students the much-cherished opportunity to come together to discuss and exchange ideas with like-minded people. Here, there's something for everyone!

Clubs

Explore our wide range of clubs

  • Boat Club

    COEP Technological University is proud to host a unique Boat Club, established in 1928, situated on the banks of the Mula River. The club boasts a diverse fleet of approximately 70 trainee and racing boats

  • Astronomy Club

    Explore the cosmos together under the guidance of passionate stargazers. From observing celestial phenomena to unraveling the mysteries of the universe, let's journey through the vastness of space as a community

  • Satellite Club(CSAT)

    Embark on an orbit of exploration with our Satellite Club! Dive into the realms of satellite technology, from understanding their functions to designing your own miniaturized space marvels. Let's launch into the future, one satellite at a time!

  • Octane Racing

    Rev up your engines and join our Racing Club for adrenaline-fueled excitement! Whether you're into fast cars, motorcycles, or even drones, our club offers thrilling competitions, expert tips, and a tight-knit community of fellow racing enthusiasts. Get ready to feel the rush of the track!

  • Robotics Study Circle(RSC)

    Where creativity meets technology! Dive into the world of robotics as we design, build, and program robots to tackle real-world challenges. From competitions to community outreach projects, join us in exploring the cutting-edge frontier of automation and artificial intelligence. Let's engineer the future, one circuit at a time!

  • Bhau's E-Cell

    Join our E-Cell and ignite your passion for innovation and business savvy! From ideation to execution, we empower aspiring entrepreneurs with workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities to turn their ideas into successful ventures. Let's embark on the journey of entrepreneurship together and shape the future of business
